- **10+ 深度Tier List** - 有数据支撑的排名

### 📝 内容优化策略

#### 1. "How-to" 标题策略（来自Polygon）
所有攻略类文章统一使用以下格式：
- How to [做某事] in [游戏名]
- How to get [物品/成就] in [游戏名]
- How to defeat [Boss] in [游戏名]

**示例：**
- How to Get Primogems Fast in Genshin Impact
- How to Rank Up Fast in Valorant
- How to Defeat the Ender Dragon in Minecraft

#### 2. FAQ格式内容结构（来自GameFAQs）
每篇攻略采用问答格式，天然符合SEO：
```markdown
# How to Get Primogems in Genshin Impact

## Q: How many Primogems can I get for free?
A: You can get approximately 60-80 Primogems daily...

## Q: What's the fastest way to farm Primogems?
A: The fastest methods include...
```

#### 3. "Updated" 实时标签（来自Polygon）
每篇文章显示更新时间，增强信任：
```
📅 Updated: 2 hours ago
✅ Verified for Patch 4.5
```
